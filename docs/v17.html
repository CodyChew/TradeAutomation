Rules Tested

  • Current V15 uses LP3, all H4/H8/H12/D1/W1, 0.5 signal-candle pullback, FS structure stop, 1R target, fixed 6-bar signal window, and fixed 6-bar pullback wait.
  • Strict touch for longs requires FS structure low <= LP support.
  • Strict touch for shorts requires FS structure high >= LP resistance.
  • Gap variants allow the structure to miss the LP by up to 0.25, 0.50, or 1.00 ATR.
  • Rows with missing or zero ATR are marked unknown and are not accepted by gap filters unless they strictly touch.

Metric Glossary

Realized DD
Closed-trade equity drawdown only. It ignores open trade risk until a position exits.
Risk-Reserved DD
Live-account stress drawdown that subtracts full open risk while positions are active.
Max Reserved Risk
Largest total open risk reserved at one time across accepted portfolio trades.
Worst Month
Largest calendar-month loss in account-percent terms for the tested schedule.
Return/DD
Total return divided by risk-reserved drawdown where available; higher is more efficient.
Avg R / PF
Average net R per trade and profit factor. Use both with trade count before trusting a row.
